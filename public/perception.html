                Visual Search Task: How to Play         
                Welcome to the Visual Search Task! This engaging game 
                challenges your ability to quickly and accurately
                find a specific target among a grid of distractors.
                 Here's how you play:

                Objective
                Your goal is to find and click on a target shape or letter 
                hidden among a grid of similar but slightly
                different shapes or letters. Speed and accuracy are key!

                How to Play
                Starting the Game:

                Once you start the game, a grid will appear on your screen 
                for a short period.
                The grid will be filled with various shapes or letters.
                Understanding the Grid:

              
                Each cell in the grid will contain either the target item 
                you need to find or a distractor that looks similar to 
                the target.
                Identifying the Target:

                Before the grid appears, you'll be informed about the target 
                shape or letter you need to find. For example, you might need
                 to find the letter "T" among a grid full of "I"s.
                Pay close attention to the target item so you can recognize 
                it quickly when the grid appears.

                Making Your Selection:

                As soon as you spot the target item, click on it as quickly as
                 possible.
                Be careful with your selection : clicking on a distractor 
                instead of the target will affect your
                accuracy.
                Feedback and Scoring:

                After you click, you will receive immediate feedback 
                indicating whether you found the correct target.
                If you clicked on the correct target,it will be red.

                Tracking Performance:

                Your performance will be tracked based on how much u pressed 
                correct targets 
                Try to improve your speed and accuracy with each attempt!
                Example
                Task: "Find the letter 'T' among a grid of 'I's."

                Starting: The game begins, and you see a grid.
                Identifying the Target: You know you need to find the letter
                 "T."
                Grid Display: The grid appears, filled with letters "I" and
                 some letter "T."
                Finding and Clicking: You scan the grid, spot the "T," and 
                click on it as fast as you can.
               
                Stay Focused: Concentrate on the grid and block out distractions.
                Be Quick but Accurate: Speed is important, but accuracy matters
                 more. Dont rush so much that you click the wrong item.
                Practice: The more you play, the better you'll get at quickly
                 identifying the target among distractors.
                Enjoy the challenge and see how fast and accurately you can find
